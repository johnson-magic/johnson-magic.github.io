🇨🇳 Biography

Johnson-magic is now a Engineer at Intsig. Johnson-magic's interests include compute system, software engineering and ai, with a focus on generic object detection, text object detection, text recognition, Vision-Language Models.

Johnson-magic received the B.E. degree from College of Information and Technology, DongHua University，which belongs to "211 Engineering" universities, China, in 2015. He received the M.S. degree from School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. His research advisor is Prof. Lanjuan Zhu.

Johnson-magic has six years work experience of computer vision algorithms. Before joining Intsig, he worked at Hikvision for two and a half years.
